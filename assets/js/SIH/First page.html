About Us

A group of people who wanted to bring forth change.

We at Sansthaein Aur Samvidhan are dedicated to making the Constitution of India accessible to everyone by simplifying key aspects of the Legislature, Executive, and Judiciary through interactive, gamified platforms. Our mission is to empower citizens with knowledge, fostering informed decisions about their rights and duties.

LEGISTLATURE This branch makes the laws. It is like a big group of elected people who come together to decide on rules everyone has to follow.
EXECUTIVE This branch enforces the laws. It is led by the President or Prime Minister, who ensures that the laws are followed and that the government runs smoothly.
JUDICIARY This branch interprets the laws. It includes judges and courts, who decide what the laws mean and make sure they are applied fairly.
